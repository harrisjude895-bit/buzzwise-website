FAQ

Questions UK trades ask before automating AR

Here’s how it works, what it connects to, and what you can expect in the first month.

Will this sound “spammy” to my customers?
No. We write the reminders to be polite and professional, with sensible spacing (day 7/14/21). You can approve tone and wording before it goes live.
What triggers the invoice automatically?
Typically a “job complete” action or stage change in your workflow. We map the trigger to how you already work, so nothing new for your team to learn.
Can you include payment links and confirmations?
Yes. We can include pay-now links where supported and send receipts/confirmation messaging when payment is received, so follow-ups stop automatically.
How long does setup take?
Most builds are set up in 5–10 days, depending on what you’re connecting and how complex your invoicing workflow is.
Is it month-to-month?
Yes, month-to-month with no lock-in. Stay because the system works, not because of a contract.
