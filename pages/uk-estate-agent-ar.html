FAQ

Invoice & AR Automation FAQ

Answers to common questions from UK estate agents, letting agencies, and property management teams.

Can this work with our current invoicing or property management system?
Yes. During setup we map your invoice fields, clients, and statuses, then connect your system via API, secure exports, or your existing workflow so invoices and follow-ups run automatically.
What happens when someone pays?
Once a payment is recorded, the reminders stop automatically and the account is marked as settled. Your team keeps a clear audit trail of every message and status change.
Can we customise the reminder tone and escalation wording?
Absolutely. We create your 7/14/21-day reminders and formal notice templates in your brand voice, then you approve them before we switch the automation on.
How fast can we go live?
Most agencies are live in 5-10 days. We’ll confirm triggers, templates, and escalation rules, then launch with monitoring so the first cycle runs smoothly.
Is it month-to-month and what’s included in the retainer?
Yes, it’s month-to-month. The retainer covers monitoring, improvements to sequences and templates, and ongoing support so your AR automation keeps performing as your portfolio grows.
